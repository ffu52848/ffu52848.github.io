Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

AI Kard is committed to protecting your personal privacy. This policy explains how we process the information generated during your use of our services to comply with Apple App Store standards.

1. Information Collection & Third-Party Processing

  • Device Identification: We use IDFA/IDFV (Identifier for Advertisers/Vendors) for AdMob advertisement optimization and application stability monitoring.
  • Third-Party AI Processing (Google Gemini): Your image data is transmitted via encrypted channels to Google Gemini AI for real-time recognition. This data is processed transiently and is NOT used for AI model training.
  • Payments & Subscriptions: All financial transactions are handled securely by the Apple App Store.

2. Data Security & Asset Protection

  • Local Storage: By default, your scanned contact information and business card images are stored locally in your device's secure database.
  • Asset Synchronization: We utilize iCloud KV and system-level Keychain technology to ensure your purchase rights are synchronized across devices.
