AI Kard 隱私權政策

最後更新日期：2026年3月13日

本應用程式（以下簡稱「我們」）致力於保護您的個人隱私。本政策說明我們如何處理您在使用服務時產生的資訊，以符合 Apple App Store 的隱私規範。

1. 資訊蒐集與第三方處理

  • 設備識別資訊： 為了提供 AdMob 廣告服務並分析 App 穩定性，我們會讀取您的裝置識別碼（如 IDFA/IDFV）。這些資訊僅用於廣告優化與性能監控。
  • 第三方 AI 處理 (Google Gemini)： 為了提供核心的名片辨識與語義分析服務，當您主動觸發「掃描」功能時，相關影像資料將透過加密傳輸至 Google Gemini AI API 進行即時處理。
    重要聲明： 傳輸之資料僅用於即時提取文字資訊，我們不允許 Google 將您的原始影像或個資用於 AI 模型訓練。辨識過程符合國際數據安全標準。
  • 支付與訂閱： 所有交易（如購買金幣或擴充功能）均由 Apple App Store 處理。我們不會接觸、蒐集或儲存您的信用卡號碼或銀行帳戶資訊。

2. 資料安全與資產保護

  • 本地化存儲： 您所辨識的名片聯絡資訊與影像，預設均儲存於您手機的本地資料庫中。我們不會將您的私密聯絡人清單上傳至我們的私有伺服器。
  • 資產同步技術 (iCloud & Keychain)： 為確保您的消費權益（如金幣餘額、已購項目），我們使用 iCloud Key-Value (KV) 與系統級 Keychain (鑰匙圈) 加密技術。這能讓您在更換設備或重裝 App 時自動恢復資產，同時防止虛假領取以維護服務公平性。

3. 使用者權利與資料刪除

  • 資料控制： 您可以隨時透過 App 內的「清空資料」功能手動移除本地所有名片紀錄。
  • 權限撤回： 您可以隨時在 iOS 設定中關閉相機、相簿或 IDFA 追蹤權限，但這可能會導致部分核心辨識功能無法運作。

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

AI Kard (hereinafter referred to as "we") is committed to protecting your personal privacy. This policy explains how we process the information generated during your use of our services to comply with Apple App Store standards.

1. Information Collection & Third-Party Processing

  • Device Identification: For AdMob advertising services and stability analysis, we read your device identifiers (such as IDFA/IDFV). This information is used only for advertisement optimization and performance monitoring.
  • Third-Party AI Processing (Google Gemini): To provide core business card recognition and semantic analysis services, relevant image data is transmitted via encrypted channels to the Google Gemini AI API for real-time processing.
    Important Declaration: The transmitted data is used solely for real-time text extraction. We DO NOT allow Google to use your raw images or personal data for AI model training. The recognition process complies with international data security standards.
  • Payments & Subscriptions: All transactions (such as purchasing coins or extensions) are handled by the Apple App Store. We do not access, collect, or store your credit card numbers or bank account information.

2. Data Security & Asset Protection

  • Local Storage: Your recognized contact information and images are stored in your device's local database by default. We do not upload your private contact list to our private servers.
  • Asset Synchronization Technology (iCloud & Keychain): To ensure your purchase rights (such as coin balances and purchased items), we utilize iCloud Key-Value (KV) and system-level Keychain encryption technology. This allows for automatic asset recovery upon device change or reinstallation while preventing fraudulent claims to maintain service fairness.

3. User Rights & Data Deletion

  • Data Control: You may manually remove all local business card records at any time via the "Clear Data" function within the App.
  • Permission Withdrawal: You may disable Camera, Photo Library, or IDFA tracking permissions in iOS settings at any time, though this may impact core recognition features.
